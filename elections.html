UTS Indian Society - DESI Elections

The elections are conducted annually by the UTS Indian Society - DESI and governed by the Elections guidelines and the President Of The UTS Indian Society - DESI.

Elections are held for

  • Office Bearer for President
  • Office Bearer for Treasurer
  • Office Bearer for Secretary

Nominations & Elections Information is communicated to members via email prior to an election.
