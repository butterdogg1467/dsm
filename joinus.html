Our Story

DSA started as a small idea on a late night Facetime call between some friends during Covid in 2020 but we had never anticipated this level of love, support and demand for our initiative. In our first year of operations, we focused on establishing an image and presence at McMaster. We managed to host two sold out events (our diwali bonfire and holi semi-formal) during that year. In our second year of running, we wanted to focus on growing our community and organizing more diverse events and this was accomplished by hosting unique events like Iftar Night, Cricket Screenings, Desi Club Nights and by being the first university in Canada to organize a Mock Mehndi Night. In our third year, our goal was to become the biggest cultural club at McMaster and to continue organizing innovative events throughout the year. We were able to achieve this goal with the help of events like Qawwali Night, Holi Sundowner Festival and Chai House. This immense love, support and demand has motivated us to expand DSA across multiple campuses in Canada and to continue to organize events that spark creativity while building an inclusive and welcoming environment for all.
