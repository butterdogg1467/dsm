FAQs

Who are we?

“DESI by #UTSIndian” is a brand of the UTS Indian Society which is a Social/Cultural club based out of UTS. The brand name DESI was created by the UTS Indian Society to target students from the entire south asian community in 2022, whether you are a local or international student, we happily welcome you with open arms. From unforgettable socials to celebrating festivals, competitive sport to Bollywood. The UTS Indian Society offers something for everyone! If you’re looking to have an amazing year at university, to form lifelong friendships, to have a place of reliable support.

Whats The Vision?

Promoting the culture & tradition of quality music, a fun & safe environment to party in and the opportunity to see the biggest and best acts on your doorstep is our philosophy! We aim to bring together people & organise the biggest social events for the students of Australia.

Why Join UTS Indian?

UTS Indian Hosts Across 5 Universities In NSW and holds a collective 150+ events throughout the year that provide a unique cultural experience to everyone.

Here’s some of our Benefits:

  • Free food from club stalls throughout the entirety of the year!
  • Expand your network and make life-long friends
  • Enjoy exclusive discounts via our Website and early access to our amazing events such as Freshers Parties, DESI Cruise and The DESI Ball.
  • To be apart of something bigger than yourself, as we strive to actively give back to the community.
